## The core math, stated plainly

The single highest-leverage number is **trial-to-paid conversion (Layer 3)**.
At today's traffic, moving from ~27% → ~50% roughly **doubles new revenue without spending a dollar more on acquisition**. Layer 2 (more trials) and Layer 4 (bigger deals) compound on top of it — but conversion is the cheapest growth we have.

## Decisions needed from leadership

> These are the unblockers. Each one is something only leadership can green-light.

1. **Budget** — approve always-on campaign spend (Layer 2) vs. event-only?
2. **Prioritization** — is conversion (Layer 3) the #1 focus for the next quarter, ahead of new top-of-funnel bets?
3. **Eng allocation** — can Andrei's Q3 time cover both attribution automation (Layer 1) *and* in-app conversion (Layer 3), or do we sequence them?
4. **Hiring** — do we need dedicated growth/marketing capacity to run more campaigns in parallel?
5. **Enterprise** — are we ready to chase whales/counties as a deliberate motion, or keep them opportunistic?
